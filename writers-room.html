Writer's Room, 2022

A discrete intervention to a Victorian Terrace; The writer’s room bookends an external courtyard, shaping the outlook from the original house and providing a designated working space away from the primary realm of the family home. The writer’s room is conceived as a simple legible structure; posts, beams and window frames are constructed as a single element, hot dipped galvanised and brought onto site as prefabricated elements. The simple solid hatches shield the functions of “work” from the main house but allow for family interactions across the courtyard.
